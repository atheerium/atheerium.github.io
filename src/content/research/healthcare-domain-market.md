## Scope

Analyzed 400+ healthcare domain sales from 2020–2026 to identify buyer patterns and pricing.

## Key findings

### Buyer types

1. **Telehealth startups** — raised $X in Series A, need a brandable .com
2. **Health SaaS companies** — B2B healthcare software
3. **Medical practices** — local clinics, dentistry, dermatology
4. **Health media / publishing**

### Price ranges

| Category | Price Range | Liquidity |
|----------|-------------|-----------|
| Short brandables (5-6L) | $2K–$8K | Medium |
| Geo + Health | $1K–$4K | Slow |
| Medical SaaS names | $3K–$15K | Medium |
| Wellness / alternative | $500–$3K | Fast |

### Premium keywords

- "Care", "Health", "Med", "Doc", "Rx", "Vital"
- Prefixes: "Get", "My", "Go"
- Suffixes: "Hub", "Pro", "Now"
