# atheerium.github.io — Product Context

## Purpose
Personal website and knowledge base. A public collection of domain investing research, freelance development insights, tools, and notes.

## Owner
Atheerium — domain investor, freelance web developer, solo builder based in Aflou, Algeria.

## Audience
- Domain investors and flippers
- Freelance web developers
- Solo builders and makers
- People interested in building in public

## Tone & Voice
- Direct, no filler, no fluff
- Knowledge-first — every page should be useful weeks after it's written
- Build-in-public transparency — write as if readers are watching
- First-person, practical, opinionated but humble

## Topics
- Domain investing (portfolio, sales, market analysis, acquisition strategies)
- Freelance development (Next.js, Supabase, Flutter, Gulf-region SMBs)
- Tools and automation (domain analysis, lead generation, content publishing)
- Market research and data analysis

## Design Register
Brand. The site IS the product — the design is the experience of reading and navigating the knowledge base.

## References / Inspiration
- sive.rs — minimal, knowledge-first personal site
- Other personal knowledge bases in the domain investing space

## What Makes This Site Different
- No database, no comments, no JavaScript framework
- Markdown-native content pipeline
- Focus on evergreen content over news/posts
- Data-driven domain investing analysis (19K+ domain sales records)
